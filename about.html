Meet Ashley

Licensed Esthetician & Founder of Rosie's Beauty Spa

The Story Behind the Spa

Rosie's Beauty Spa was born from love — named after Ashley's grandmother Rosie, whose warmth and wisdom shaped the woman Ashley became. When Ashley opened the doors in August 2019, she had a vision: to build a space where Black and brown women could walk in and know — instantly — that every product, every treatment, and every technique was made with their skin in mind.

Over 6 years and 800+ clients later, that vision is fully alive inside Pioneer Plaza in Arlington, TX. Ashley is a Licensed Esthetician with deep expertise in corrective skincare for melanin-rich skin. She's helped clients heal hyperpigmentation, restore glow after years of one-size-fits-all treatments, and finally feel at home in their own skin.

Her approach is rooted in education. Before she ever picks up a tool, she's listening — to your history, your goals, your concerns. Because no two complexions are the same, and no protocol should be either.

"No one-size-fits-all here. Your melanin deserves a treatment built specifically for you."

Whether you're coming in for your first facial, exploring microneedling, or in need of a deep corrective treatment, you'll leave with knowledge about your skin — not just a glow. That's the Rosie's promise.
