Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Rosie's Beauty Spa ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at rosiesbeautyspa.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or book services with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Form Information: When you submit a contact form, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and any message you provide. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Booking Information (via PocketSuite): When you book a service, you are directed to PocketSuite, a third-party booking platform. Information collected during booking — including your name, email, phone number, and payment details — is governed by PocketSuite's own privacy policy. We receive confirmation data from PocketSuite to manage your appointment.
  • Usage Data (via Google Analytics): We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymized information about how visitors interact with our website, including pages visited, time spent, device type, and general location. This data is used to improve our website and marketing.
  • Advertising Data (via Meta/Facebook Pixel): We may use the Meta Pixel to measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns on Facebook and Instagram. This may include anonymized conversion data.

2. Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to support analytics and advertising functionality:

  • Google Analytics cookies track anonymized website usage to help us understand visitor behavior.
  • Meta Pixel cookies may be set if you arrive from or interact with our Facebook or Instagram ads.

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on. You can manage your Meta ad preferences at facebook.com/ads/preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer service
  • Manage appointment bookings and send reminders
  • Improve our website content and user experience
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • PocketSuite — online booking and appointment management (pocketsuite.io/privacy)
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — website analytics (policies.google.com/privacy)
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) — advertising and conversion tracking (facebook.com/policy)
  • Google Maps — embedded location map

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the information below.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
